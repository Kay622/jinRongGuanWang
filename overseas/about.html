关于锦程

ABOUT

锦程海外是一支以成熟的经验，专业的服务，诚信的态度为帮助客户实现全球资产配置财富规划的全方位海外规划服务团队。作为为中国高净值家庭的全球协调人，团队会根据客户自身情况定制一系列海外规划方案， 包括移民身份规划、海外置业投资规划、跨境税务及子女海外教育规划、海外生活保障、安家服务规划等等，提供专业、全面、立体化的全方位海外规划服务。

Jincheng Overseas is an all-round overseas planning service team with mature experience, professional service and honest attitude to help customers realize global asset allocation wealth planning. As a global coordinator for high-net-worth families in China, the team will customize a series of overseas planning solutions based on the client's own situation, including immigration status planning, overseas investment planning, cross-border taxation and overseas education planning for children, overseas living security, and home security planning. In addition, we provide professional, comprehensive and three-dimensional all-round overseas planning services.
