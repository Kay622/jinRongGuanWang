马耳他：国债投资居留计划

Malta: Treasury investment and residence program

项目简介：地中海的天堂岛国，英国的后花园，欧洲的“新加坡”，是欧洲人热爱的旅游度假胜地之一；四位一体的国家， 欧盟成员国、欧元区、申根国、英联邦的所属国；申请人需要25万欧元及以上的国债，并持有五年； 同时申请人需要购买或租赁符合政府要求的房产，并持有五年；即可实现一家四代移民

项目优势/亮点：
1.一家四代，无经商、语言、学历背景要求，门槛低；零移民监
2.先获批后投资A级国债，安全性高，操作流程简单,获批有保障
3.投资方式灵活，可全款可融资，可买房可租房，尽享超高性价比
4.英语国家，英语普及率欧洲第四，交流无障碍，融入简单
5.纯正英式教育，入学方便，学费低廉
6.医疗水平高，世界排名第五
7.全球唯一四位一体，享受各种厉害关税贸易优惠、畅游申根国家
8.发达国家，经济稳健发展，利商型政府，绝佳的投资和经商环境，就业前景广阔

Project advantages / highlights
1. A fourth-generation family with no business, language or educational background requirements and low threshold;Zero immigration
2. First approved and then invested in a-grade national debt, with high security, simple operation process and guaranteed approval
3. Flexible investment method, can be fully financed, can buy a house or rent a house, enjoy the ultra high cost performance
4. English-speaking countries, with the fourth highest English penetration rate in Europe, enjoy barrier-free communication and easy integration
5. Pure British education, convenient entrance and low tuition
6. High medical level, ranking the fifth in the world
7. The only four-in-one in the world, enjoying various severe tariff and trade preferences and visiting schengen countries
8. Developed countries have sound economic development, pro-business government, excellent investment and business environment, and broad employment prospects


申请条件：
1.25万欧元及以上的国债，并持有五年
2.购买或租赁符合政府要求的房产，并持有五年
3.无犯罪记录
4.年收入超过10万欧元或固定资产超过50万欧元
5.购买医疗保险

application requirement
1. Government bonds of 250,000 euros or more and held for five years
2. Buy or rent a property that meets government requirements and hold it for five years
3. No criminal record
4. Annual income over 100,000 euros or fixed assets over 500,000 euros
5. Buy health insurance
