塞浦路斯：护照项目

Cyprus: passport item

项目简介：位于亚非欧的交界处，欧洲的心脏，地中海式气候，拥有55块蓝旗沙滩，是自然爱好者的圣地；免签178个国家，全球最佳经商的国家，欧盟最低税制；法律体系完备，避税的天堂

项目优势/亮点：
1.社会安全性高、法律体系健全、税制优惠、多元产业
2.税率水平较低的国家，拥有多项税收优惠制度
3.免签178个国家：包括加拿大、新西兰、新加坡、日本等等
4.全家自由定居欧盟国家、享受欧盟福利
5.财富管理规划，资本进入欧洲的便捷通道
6.欧洲最快、最简单、性价比最高获批护照的国家

Project advantages / highlights
1. High social security, sound legal system, preferential tax system and diversified industries
2. Countries with lower tax rates have multiple preferential tax systems
3. Visa-free 178 countries: including Canada, New Zealand, Singapore, Japan and so on
4. Family members are free to settle in eu countries and enjoy eu benefits
5. Wealth management planning, convenient access to capital in Europe
6. The fastest, simplest and most cost-effective passport approved country in Europe


申请条件：
1.非欧盟公民
2.年满18周岁
3.无犯罪记录
4.购买商业保险
5.购买净价值至少200万欧元的一套或多套自住房产

application requirement
1. Non-eu citizens
2. Be at least 18 years old
3. No criminal record
4. Buy commercial insurance
5. Purchase one or more owner-occupied homes with a net value of at least 2 million euros
