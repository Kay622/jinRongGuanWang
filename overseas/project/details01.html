泰国

Thailand

项目简介：拉亚度假酒店项目，背山面海，国家级海岸生活保护区，与众多世界五星级酒店共筑顶级阵容； 与安纳塔拉度假村为邻，共享安达曼海上最美的落日；原生自然的五星级酒店，远离喧嚣世界；温德姆酒店集团五星级托管运营，全程投资保障。

项目优势/亮点：
1.项目位于普吉岛西海岸，高端奢华度假区-拉妍海滩，周边环绕众多高端五星级酒店
2.国家级海岸生活保护区，比邻国家级自然保护区，奢享原生自然环境
3.周边配套设施完善，米其林餐厅、五星级酒店、SPA中心，全天候餐厅、健身中心等
4.由美国温德姆酒店托管运营，拎包入住，总价低，投资回报率稳定

Project advantages / highlights
1. The project is located in la yan beach, a high-end luxury resort on the west coast of phuket island, surrounded by many high-end five-star hotels
2. National coastal life reserve, adjacent to national nature reserve, enjoys the original natural environment
3. The surrounding supporting facilities are perfect, including Michelin restaurant, five-star hotel, SPA center, all-weather restaurant, fitness center, etc
4. Managed and operated by wyndham hotel in the United States, with low total price and stable return on investment


购房办理流程：
1.国内支付预定金2万人民币，锁定房源
2.客户登陆泰国考察，签署预订单
3.回国后完成购房协议的签署
4.分期将款项打至开发商账户
5.办理房屋过户手续
6.准备收房，办理包租事宜

Purchase process
1. Pay a deposit of RMB 20,000 in China to lock the house
2. Visit Thailand and sign the booking form
3. Completed the signing of the house purchase agreement after returning to China
4. Transfer the payment to the developer's account in installments
5. House transfer procedures
6. Prepare to take over the house and handle leasing matters
