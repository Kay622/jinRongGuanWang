伦敦麦迪逊项目

London Madison project

项目简介：位于伦敦金丝雀码头，伦敦的“动脉”，欧洲增长最快的商业区，三条轨道交通交汇，高层视野宽阔，坐观世界金融中心与泰晤士河景，伦敦住宅新坐标

项目优势/亮点：
1.英国房地产市场成熟稳定且规范，具有较强的流动性，住宅供不应求
2.宽松的房产政策，低首付、低利率、持有成本低、租金回报率高
3.拥有全球最佳的教育资源，被誉为“全球最好的大学城市”
4.对海外投资者没有限购，投资安全，风险低

Project advantages / highlights
1. The UK real estate market is mature, stable and standardized, with strong liquidity, and the demand for housing is in short supply
2. Loose real estate policy, low down payment, low interest rate, low holding cost and high rental rate
3. It has the best educational resources in the world and is honored as "the best university city in the world".
4. There is no limit for overseas investors, so the investment is safe and the risk is low


购房办理流程：
1.选购房产，签署deal sheet
2.签署PSA，支付首款
3.办理贷款（依情况而定）
4.补齐尾款，办理过户
5.入住

Purchase process
1. Buy the property and sign the deal sheet
2. Sign PSA and pay the first payment
3. Loan processing (as the case may be)
4. Complete balance payment and transact the transfer
5. Check in
