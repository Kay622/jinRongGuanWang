纽约布鲁克林东河湾项目

East bend, brooklyn, New York

项目简介：项目地处布鲁克林区南威廉斯堡肯特街区，纽约CBD，西邻华尔街、纽约大学、唐人街；纽约热门商区，艺术家、时尚者的聚集地，个性十足的区域，纽约地产的潜力股

项目优势/亮点：
1.纽约的热门住宅区，临近华尔街，交通设施便利
2.周边配套设施完善，文化产业和时尚人群的聚集地，教育设施完备，临近世界顶尖高校
3.区域政策利好，享受纽约421A减税政策，投资升值空间大
4.美式建筑风格，世界顶尖级设计团队打造，配套设施完善，休闲设施丰富
5.精装修，拎包入住，投资回报率稳定

Project advantages / highlights
1. New York is a hot residential area near Wall Street with convenient transportation
2. Perfect supporting facilities, gathering place of cultural industry and fashion crowd, complete educational facilities, close to the world's top universities
3. Favorable regional policies, enjoying New York 421A tax reduction policy, and large space for investment appreciation
4. American architectural style, built by the world's top design team, complete supporting facilities and rich leisure facilities
5. Fine decoration, move-in, stable return on investment

3.办理贷款（依情况而定）
4.补齐尾款，办理过户
5.入住

1. Buy the property and sign the deal sheet
2. Sign PSA and pay the first payment
3. Loan processing (as the case may be)
4. Complete balance payment and transact the transfer
5. Check in
