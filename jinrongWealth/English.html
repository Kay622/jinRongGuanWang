Service system

  • Asset Management

    High net worth investors and their families, the effective management of assets is the primary task. However, in order to manage a large-scale, in addition to having a complete asset allocation, it is also necessary to ensure the stable increase of assets.

  • Tax planning

    From the personal income tax of family members to the business income tax of family enterprises, it is the scope of tax planning that needs to be fully covered. In addition, there are also tax issues such as inheritance and gifts between families, and it is even more necessary to plan early.

  • Insurance and Trust

    The inheritance and distribution of family assets is the most controversial. Once the prior integration is ignored, the direct consequence is the rapid loss of assets; Proper insurance and trust planning can reduce the probability of such risks occurring.

  • Family education for future generations

    Sustainable management is the ultimate dream of family business. It is an important topic for the family office to help cultivate the ability of the next generation to become a family business and provide opportunities to learn how to handle related issues.
